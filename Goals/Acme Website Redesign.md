# Acme Website Redesign

E-commerce website redesign project for Acme Corp focused on improving mobile conversion rates and modernizing their brand.

## Objectives

- Increase mobile conversion rate from 2.1% to 4%+
- Reduce checkout abandonment from 67% to under 40%
- Improve page load time to under 2 seconds
- Modern, mobile-first design
